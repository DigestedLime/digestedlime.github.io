Creation of Sulfuric Acid

Demonstrating the role of equilibrium in the creation of sulfuric acid.


Uses of Sulfuric Acid

  • Used to make phosphoric acid
  • Used to make ammonium sulfate
  • Used in metal processing
  • Used to make caprolactam
  • Used to make hydrofluoric acid and phenol with propanone

Global production

Location Amount Produced
World 231 M tonnes
China 74 M tonnes
United States 37 M tonnes
India 16 M tonnes
Russia 14 M tonnes
Morocco 7 M tonnes
These figures are from 2011 - 2012. It has been projected that in 2018, the world production would be around 260 M tonnes, and continued to increase until 2023.

Manufacturing Process

The entire process happens in 4 steps:

  1. Extraction of sulfur
  2. Creation of Sulfur Dioxide
  3. Creation of Sulfur Trioxide
  4. Creation of Sulfur Acid

Random information about Sulfuric acid

  • Production of Sulfuric Acid was linked to the industrial power of a nation
  • The creation of Sulfuric Acid is sometimes referred to as the Contact Process
