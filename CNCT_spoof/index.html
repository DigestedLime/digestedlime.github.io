ABOUT US

Connecting Youth, Connecting You.

CNCT Tutors is a start-up company in Toronto, Canada, dedicated to making education accessible for the leaders of tomorrow. Through a commitment to youth education, CNCT Tutors will lead the way by providing a service that empowers youth to learn more.

The CNCT Tutors. Logo!


Welcome to our website. We are a group of entrepreneurial high school students striving to eliminate rates of youth dropouts in our education. CNCT Tutors offers a service that connects youth to mentors that help them further their own learning. We have partnered with Charity Intelligence Canada, a North American Organization that works to help youth get back on their feet, and 700$ of our profit will go to them.
